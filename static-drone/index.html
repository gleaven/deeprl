How to teach the drone
  1. Click FLY to take manual control
  2. Use keyboard to fly: WS pitch, AD roll, SPACESHIFT throttle, QE yaw
  3. Click REC to start recording your flight
  4. Fly the demonstration, then click STOP
  5. Enter a name and click SAVE
  6. Click FLY again to exit manual mode
  7. Click PRETRAIN to teach the model your demos
  8. After pretraining, click START — the model begins from your behavior and improves beyond it via RL
